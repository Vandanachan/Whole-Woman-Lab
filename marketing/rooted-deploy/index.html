No. 01 · Your Constitution

Every woman has a Constitution.

Not because you didn't try hard enough. Because almost everything handed to you — the supplement stack, the elimination diet, the influencer's morning routine — was designed for an average body. Not yours.

In Traditional Chinese Medicine it's your pattern — Qi, Blood, Yin, Yang, damp, heat, stagnation. In Ayurveda it's your dosha — Vata, Pitta, Kapha. Two languages describing the same thing: the underlying blueprint that decides how you digest, sleep, cycle, recover from stress, and respond to food, weather and the seasons.

Most wellness advice never asks what your constitution is. It hands you someone else's template and calls it a protocol. Whole Woman Academy starts from the opposite direction — your body first, then the plan.

No. 05 · Before You Go

Questions worth asking.

I already took the free Constitution Mini Quiz. Why do I need this?

The free quiz gives you a snapshot. Whole Woman Academy gives you the full diagnostic report, then a whole year of watching that pattern move with your seasons, cycle and life — plus the monthly class and community to actually act on it.

I don't know anything about TCM or Ayurveda. Is this over my head?

No — everything is explained in plain language as you go. You don't need to arrive knowing the terms; that's what the monthly classes are for.

What's the difference between Root Cause Club and Whole Woman Academy?

Root Cause Club is the monthly membership on its own. Whole Woman Academy is the full year, with your Integrated Constitution Report and progress tracking built in from day one — it's a different, more complete commitment, not just a longer subscription.

Is this a replacement for seeing a doctor?

No. Whole Woman Academy is educational — it teaches you to understand your own constitution and apply food, herb and lifestyle strategy around it. It is not a diagnosis of disease and does not replace medical care. Please continue working with your physician for any medical concern, and always disclose new supplements or herbs to your care team.

What if I want more direct, personal support?

That's what the Whole Woman Diagnostic™ and Whole Woman Clinical Partnership™ are for — a deeper 1:1 diagnostic experience and ongoing personal guidance. Many Whole Woman Academy members go on to book one of those when they're ready for that level of attention.
